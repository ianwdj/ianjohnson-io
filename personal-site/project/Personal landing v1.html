01About

I build AI products that move the top line. Right now that means Aida, a seed-stage company figuring out what revenue intelligence looks like when meeting agents are real.

Before Aida I founded Lasso AI for the creator economy, bootstrapped to acquisition. Earlier I ran product at Flow through a $500M acquisition by Global-e, and led product at Showtime Analytics, an Alibaba Pictures joint venture that scaled into 26 countries.

Outside work I co-founded a natural wine bar and a coffee shop. I read, I watch, I keep too many lists.
